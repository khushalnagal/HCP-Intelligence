PHARMA HCP PRESCRIBER INTELLIGENCE - BUSINESS INSIGHT SUMMARY
======================================================================

OVERVIEW
This analysis segments 569,119 cardiovascular prescribers from
857,756 cleaned Medicare Part D records to identify high-priority
sales targets across 6 territories.

DATASET
  Raw records ingested   : 1,000,000
  Records after cleaning : 857,756 (14.2% removed - nulls, suppressed, invalid)
  Unique HCPs analyzed   : 569,119

SEGMENTATION (KMeans, k=4, silhouette=0.40)
  High Priority : 199,553 HCPs - $8.5B (71% of spend)
  Growth        : 67,859 HCPs
  Maintenance   : 192,350 HCPs
  Dormant       : 109,357 HCPs

FINDINGS
  1. Revenue concentration: the High Priority segment (199,553 doctors,
     35% of the base) accounts for 71% of total spend.
     Sales effort should concentrate here first.

  2. Within High Priority, 52,888 doctors show a growing prescribing trend
     and 49,332 show a declining trend. These require different rep actions -
     accelerate visits for growing accounts, protect relationships for declining ones.

  3. Win-back opportunity: 9,117 dormant high-value doctors represent
     $115M in realistic recoverable volume, based on a
     specialty- and recency-adjusted recovery rate (not raw historical volume).

RECOMMENDATION
  Prioritize territory call plans using the High Priority segment first,
  layering in trend direction to decide cadence. Route the 9,117 win-back
  candidates to a separate re-engagement track rather than standard rep visits.
